A simpler way of binding those key events!

Tie your key-events to the very keys you want to listen on. $( selector ).qeydown( 'ctrl+s', handler ); is much more readable than $( selector ).keydown(function(e) { if(e.ctrlKey && e.which === 83) });!

What do you prefer?

$( 'body' ).keypress(function( e ){
  if(!e.ctrlKey || !e.which === 83 ) return;
  saveDocument();
});
	    
$( 'body' ).qeypress( 'ctrl+s', saveDocument );
